<p><strong>The One-dimensional Diagnostic Ultrasound Devices Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>A Type</li><li>M Type</li><li>D Type</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>One-dimensional diagnostic ultrasound devices are categorized into several types based on their imaging capabilities and applications. </p><p>- **A-Type** (Amplitude modulation) provides simple one-dimensional displays of echoes, primarily used in ophthalmology to measure eye structures. </p><p>- **M-Type** (Motion mode) visualizes moving structures like the heart, capturing motion over time, useful in cardiology. </p><p>- **D-Type** (Doppler) focuses on velocity measurements, assessing blood flow and heart function. </p><p>- **Others** encompass advanced hybrid and specialized devices tailored for niche applications, enhancing diagnostic capabilities.</p></p>
