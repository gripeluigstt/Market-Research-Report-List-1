<p><p>Vehicle toll collection and access systems can be categorized into three main types: Mainline Barrier Tolls, Entry/Exit Tolls, and Others. Mainline Barrier Tolls involve fixed toll booths where vehicles stop to pay, often found on highways. Entry/Exit Tolls charge vehicles based on entrances and exits used, commonly seen in urban expressways. The "Others" category encompasses various innovative systems like automated tolling or congestion pricing technologies, designed to manage traffic and enhance revenue without traditional barriers.</p></p>
