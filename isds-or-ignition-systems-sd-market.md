<p><p>Ignition systems (ISDs) are critical components in explosive applications, categorized mainly into single-pulse and multiple-pulse initiation types. Single-pulse initiation systems deliver a single, powerful signal to trigger detonation, making them suitable for straightforward applications. In contrast, multiple-pulse initiation systems employ a sequence of signals to achieve precise timing and synchronization, enhancing control over explosive events. These distinctions influence their adoption across industries like mining, construction, and demolition, where safety and efficiency are paramount.</p></p>
