<p><p>The Polystyrene Resin Recycling Market encompasses two primary types: post-consumer recycling and post-industrial recycling. Post-consumer recycling involves collecting and reprocessing polystyrene products discarded by consumers, such as packaging and containers, turning them back into reusable materials. In contrast, post-industrial recycling focuses on reclaiming polystyrene waste generated during manufacturing processes before it reaches the consumer, enabling more efficient material recovery. Both types contribute significantly to sustainability efforts, reducing landfill waste and promoting circular economy practices.</p></p>
