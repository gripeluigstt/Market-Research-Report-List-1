The market is segmented into three main types: Monopole Electrosurgical Units, which utilize a single electrode for cutting and coagulation; Bipolar Electrosurgical Units, where two electrodes grasp tissue for precise cutting and coagulation; and Vessel Sealing Units, designed to permanently seal blood vessels with minimal thermal damage. Each type offers distinct advantages tailored to specific surgical needs.</p></p>
