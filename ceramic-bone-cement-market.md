<p><p>The ceramic bone cement market is categorized into low, medium, and high viscosity cements, each tailored for specific surgical applications. Low viscosity cements facilitate easy handling and flow, making them ideal for intricate procedures and enhancing contact with bone surfaces. Medium viscosity cements offer a balance between handling and stability, suitable for various orthopedic surgeries. High viscosity cements, with their thicker consistency, provide strong mechanical support and are often used in load-bearing applications, ensuring optimal performance in bone fixation.</p></p>
