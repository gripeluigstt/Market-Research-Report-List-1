<p><p>Digital video advertising encompasses various formats, primarily including in-stream and out-stream video ads. In-stream video ads play within streaming content, such as before, during, or after videos, capturing viewers' attention while they consume media. Conversely, out-stream video ads are designed to appear outside traditional video content, often embedded within articles or displayed as standalone units. Both types engage audiences differently, with in-stream ads benefiting from audience immersion, while out-stream ads effectively reach users in diverse browsing contexts.</p></p>
