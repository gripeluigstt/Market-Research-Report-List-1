<p><ul><li>Femtocell</li><li>Pico Cell</li><li>Micro Cell</li><li>Macro Cell</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The 5G infrastructure market comprises several cell types catering to different coverage and capacity needs. Femtocells are compact, low-power devices designed for home or small office use, enhancing indoor connectivity. Picocells serve larger areas, such as malls or offices, providing higher capacity than femtocells. Microcells cover larger areas, like urban environments, improving network density. Macrocells are high-power towers serving extensive regions, forming the backbone of the 5G network. Each cell type plays a distinct role in ensuring effective and efficient mobile communication.</p></p>
