<p><strong>The Machine Vision CMOS Image Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rolling Shutter</li><li>Global Shutter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Machine Vision CMOS Image Sensor market is primarily divided into two types: Rolling Shutter and Global Shutter. Rolling Shutter captures images line by line, which can lead to distortions in fast-moving scenes as some parts of the image are recorded at different times. In contrast, Global Shutter captures the entire frame simultaneously, eliminating motion artifacts, making it ideal for high-speed applications. Each type caters to specific needs in industrial automation, robotics, and quality inspection, affecting performance and application suitability.</p></p>
