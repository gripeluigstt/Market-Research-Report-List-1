<p><strong>The Packer Testing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Packer Hydraulic Conductivity Test</li><li>Hydraulic Conductivity Testing of Double Packers</li><li>Withdrawal Packer Testing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Packer testing encompasses various methods to evaluate hydraulic conductivity in subsurface formations. The Single Packer Hydraulic Conductivity Test utilizes a single packer to isolate a section of borehole, allowing assessments of surrounding soil or rock properties. In contrast, the Hydraulic Conductivity Testing of Double Packers employs two packers for more precise measurement across distinct intervals, enhancing accuracy. Withdrawal Packer Testing involves creating a vacuum to extract water for analysis, which helps determine aquifer characteristics and influences groundwater management strategies effectively.</p></p>
