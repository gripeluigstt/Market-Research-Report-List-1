<p><p>Industrial control transformers are specialized transformers designed to provide reduced voltage for control circuits in industrial applications. They play a crucial role in energy distribution by stepping down high primary voltage to a safer, lower secondary voltage used for operating control devices, sensors, and relays in factories and facilities. These transformers enhance equipment longevity and operational safety.</p><p>The Industrial Control Transformers Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 6.1% during the forecast period.
