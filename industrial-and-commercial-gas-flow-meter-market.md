<p><p>The industrial and commercial gas flow meter market comprises various types, primarily including diaphragm gas flow meters and rotary gas flow meters. Diaphragm gas flow meters utilize a flexible diaphragm to measure gas flow accurately, making them suitable for low-flow applications. In contrast, rotary gas flow meters operate by capturing gas in chambers and measuring rotational displacement, offering precision for higher flow rates. Both types serve crucial roles in monitoring and controlling gas consumption across various industrial and commercial sectors.</p></p>
