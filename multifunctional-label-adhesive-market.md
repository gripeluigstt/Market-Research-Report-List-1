<p><ul><li>Water-based Label Adhesive</li><li>Solvent-based Label Adhesive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The multifunctional label adhesive market primarily consists of two types: water-based and solvent-based adhesives. Water-based label adhesives are eco-friendly, offering low volatile organic compounds (VOCs) and easier cleanup, making them ideal for sustainable applications. In contrast, solvent-based label adhesives provide stronger bonding capabilities and greater resistance to moisture and temperature fluctuations, making them suitable for demanding environments. Both types cater to various industries, including food, pharmaceuticals, and retail, where label performance and functionality are crucial.</p></p>
