<p><strong>The Interior Stain Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Oil-Based Stain</li><li>Water-Based Stain</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The interior stain market is primarily divided into oil-based and water-based stains. Oil-based stains provide rich color and deep penetration, enhancing the wood grain while offering durability and resistance to wear. They, however, have longer drying times and strong odors. In contrast, water-based stains are known for their quick drying properties, low VOC emissions, and ease of cleanup, making them environmentally friendly. They deliver consistent color and clarity but may require more coats to achieve desired depth.</p></p>
