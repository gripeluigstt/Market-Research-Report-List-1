<p><p>The wheel speed sensor market, particularly for ABS applications, consists mainly of two types: Magnetic Electric Type and Hall Effect Type. Magnetic Electric sensors utilize magnetic fields to generate an output signal based on wheel rotation, while Hall Effect sensors detect changes in magnetic fields to provide precise speed information. Both types play crucial roles in enhancing vehicle safety by enabling anti-lock braking systems to function effectively, preventing wheel lock-up during braking and improving vehicle stability and control.</p></p>
