<p><p>The disposable cystoscope market consists of two main types: rigid and flexible cystoscopes. Rigid cystoscopes, typically used in surgical settings, feature a solid structure that provides excellent visibility for bladder inspections but may cause discomfort. In contrast, flexible cystoscopes are designed for patient comfort and can navigate tight spaces within the urinary tract, making them ideal for outpatient procedures. Both types cater to the rising demand for infection control and patient safety by eliminating the need for reprocessing and sterilization.</p></p>
