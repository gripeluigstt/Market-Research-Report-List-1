<p><p>The Smart Window Film market is divided into two main types: self-adhesive film and non-adhesive film. Self-adhesive films come with a sticky backing that allows for easy installation on existing glass surfaces, making them suitable for retrofitting. In contrast, non-adhesive films require a more complex installation method, often needing professional application and specialized tools. Both types offer benefits such as privacy, temperature control, and energy efficiency, catering to various consumer preferences and installation requirements in residential and commercial spaces.</p></p>
