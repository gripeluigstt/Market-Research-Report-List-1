<p><strong>The Rigid Polyimide Foam Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>"Open Foam"</li><li>"Closed Foam"</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The rigid polyimide foam market is segmented into two primary types: open foam and closed foam. Open foam features interconnected cells, resulting in higher breathability and sound absorption, making it suitable for applications requiring thermal insulation and lightweight structures. In contrast, closed foam consists of tightly packed cells that are impermeable, providing superior insulation and moisture resistance. This type is often used in demanding environments where durability and thermal performance are crucial, such as aerospace and industrial applications.</p></p>
