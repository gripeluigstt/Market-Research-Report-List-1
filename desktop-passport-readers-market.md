<p><p>Desktop passport readers are categorized into several market types based on their technology and functionality. MSI (Magnetic Stripe Interface) readers capture data from magnetic strips on passports. RFID (Radio Frequency Identification) readers utilize radio waves to scan the embedded chips in e-passports for quick data retrieval. OCR (Optical Character Recognition) readers focus on reading printed information, converting it to digital format. Other types may include barcode scanners and multi-function devices combining several technologies, enhancing data accuracy and efficiency in passport verification processes.</p></p>
