<p><p>The Off-Road Vehicle Xenon Lamps market is categorized into two main types based on the igniter configuration: Built-In Igniter and External Igniter. Built-In Igniter lamps integrate the igniter within the lamp assembly, simplifying installation and reducing space requirements. In contrast, External Igniter lamps house the igniter separately, allowing for more flexibility in placement but requiring additional installation considerations. Both types enhance visibility and performance in off-road conditions, catering to varying preferences of vehicle owners and aftermarket modifications.</p></p>
