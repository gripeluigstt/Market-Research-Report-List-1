<p><p>The Esophageal Video Capsule Endoscopy market is primarily divided into two types based on the photosensitive chip technology used: CMOS (Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor) and CCD (Charge-Coupled Device) chips. CMOS chips offer advantages such as lower power consumption, faster processing speeds, and miniaturization, enhancing the ease of swallowing the capsule. In contrast, CCD chips are known for superior image quality and higher sensitivity, making them suitable for detailed diagnostic imaging. Both technologies contribute significantly to advancements in non-invasive gastrointestinal diagnostics.</p></p>
