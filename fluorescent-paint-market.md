<p><p>The fluorescent paint market is divided into two main types: organic and inorganic fluorescent paints. Organic fluorescent paints are derived from carbon-based compounds and are known for their vibrant colors and high brightness, often used in artistic applications and signage. In contrast, inorganic fluorescent paints utilize mineral-based formulations, providing durability and UV resistance, making them ideal for industrial applications and outdoor use. Both types serve distinct purposes, catering to various sectors including art, construction, and safety.</p></p>
