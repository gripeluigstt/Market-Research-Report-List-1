<p><p>The Shield Tunnel Boring Machine (TBM) market comprises various types tailored for specific excavation needs. The Single Shield TBM features a protective shield while allowing soil removal from the front. The Double Shield TBM includes a second shield for both excavation and installation of precast lining. Gripper TBMs use hydraulic grippers to anchor against the tunnel walls for stability. Slurry TBMs employ a pressurized slurry to stabilize the tunnel face and transport excavated material. Other TBM types cater to specialized requirements in tunneling projects.</p></p>
