<p><strong>The Natural Dissolvable Sutures Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Catgut Plain Sutures</li><li>Catgut Chromic Sutures</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Natural Dissolvable Sutures market primarily includes Catgut Plain Sutures and Catgut Chromic Sutures. Catgut Plain Sutures are made from natural sheep or goat intestinal fibers and dissolve quickly, making them suitable for applications requiring rapid tissue healing. In contrast, Catgut Chromic Sutures are treated with chromium salt to enhance their durability and slow down absorption, providing longer support for tissues that heal at a slower rate. Both types cater to various surgical procedures, prioritizing safety and biocompatibility.</p></p>
