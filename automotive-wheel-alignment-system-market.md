<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Automotive Wheel Alignment System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Toe- In / Toe-Out Alignment System</li><li>Camber Alignment System</li><li>Caster Alignment System</li><li>Four Wheel Alignment System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive wheel alignment system market comprises several key types, each designed to optimize vehicle handling and tire wear. The Toe-In/Toe-Out Alignment System adjusts the angle of the wheels relative to the vehicle's centerline, enhancing steering response. The Camber Alignment System focuses on the vertical tilt of the wheels, impacting grip and stability. The Caster Alignment System, which adjusts the angle of the steering axis, influences steering effort and directional stability. Finally, the Four Wheel Alignment System encompasses all adjustments, ensuring comprehensive alignment for improved performance and safety.</p></p>
