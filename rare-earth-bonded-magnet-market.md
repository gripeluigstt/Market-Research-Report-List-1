<p><p>The rare-earth bonded magnet market primarily consists of two types: NdFeB (Neodymium-Iron-Boron) magnets and SmCo (Samarium-Cobalt) magnets. NdFeB magnets are known for their exceptional strength and are widely used in applications requiring high performance, such as electric vehicles and renewable energy systems. SmCo magnets, while generally more expensive, offer greater thermal stability and corrosion resistance, making them suitable for high-temperature applications and harsh environments. Together, these magnet types drive innovation across various industries.</p></p>
