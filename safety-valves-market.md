<p><strong>The Safety Valves Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Surface Safety Valves</li><li>Subsurface Safety Valves</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The safety valves market is primarily categorized into surface safety valves and subsurface safety valves. Surface safety valves are installed above ground, protecting production and storage facilities by automatically shutting down in case of abnormal pressure or flow conditions. In contrast, subsurface safety valves are located below the surface, particularly in oil and gas wells, providing additional safety by preventing uncontrolled fluid flow from the reservoir. Both types are essential for ensuring operational safety and minimizing environmental risks in energy production.</p></p>
