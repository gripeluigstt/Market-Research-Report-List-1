<p><p>The PE compounds market is categorized primarily into three types: HDPE, XLPE, and other variants. HDPE (high-density polyethylene) is known for its strength and rigidity, making it ideal for applications such as containers and pipes. XLPE (cross-linked polyethylene) offers enhanced thermal stability and chemical resistance, commonly used in electrical insulation and automotive applications. Other market types may include LLDPE (linear low-density polyethylene) and MDPE (medium-density polyethylene), each serving unique functions in packaging, construction, and various industrial applications.</p></p>
