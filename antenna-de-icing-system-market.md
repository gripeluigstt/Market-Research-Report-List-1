<p><p>The Antenna De-Icing System market comprises two primary types: Active De-Icing Systems and Passive De-Icing Systems. Active De-Icing Systems utilize heating elements or electrical currents to prevent ice accumulation on antennas, ensuring optimal performance in harsh weather conditions. In contrast, Passive De-Icing Systems rely on design features and materials that reduce ice adhesion and accumulation without active heating. Both systems are essential for maintaining antenna functionality in cold environments, with applications in telecommunications, aviation, and other critical sectors.</p></p>
