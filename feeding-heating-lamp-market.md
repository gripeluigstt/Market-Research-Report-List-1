<p><ul><li>Infrared</li><li>Ceramics</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Feeding Heating Lamp market comprises primarily two types: infrared and ceramic lamps. Infrared lamps emit radiant heat, providing instant warmth and energy efficiency, making them ideal for livestock and poultry applications. They are known for their ability to penetrate and warm the animals directly. On the other hand, ceramic heating lamps produce consistent, long-lasting heat without emitting light, ensuring a stable environment for young animals, particularly in breeding and rearing settings. Both types enhance animal welfare and growth efficiency.</p></p>
