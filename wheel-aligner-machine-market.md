<p><strong>The Wheel Aligner Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CCD Wheel Aligner</li><li>3D Wheel Aligner</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wheel aligner machine market comprises two main types: CCD wheel aligners and 3D wheel aligners. CCD wheel aligners utilize charge-coupled device technology to capture alignment data through cameras and targets, offering reliable measurements for vehicle alignment. In contrast, 3D wheel aligners employ advanced imaging technology to provide real-time, three-dimensional measurements, enhancing accuracy and efficiency. Both types cater to the automotive service industry, ensuring optimal vehicle performance and tire longevity by precisely aligning wheels.</p></p>
