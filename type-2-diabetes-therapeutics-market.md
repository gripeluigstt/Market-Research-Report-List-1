<p><p>The Type 2 Diabetes Therapeutics market includes several key drug classes. DPP-4 inhibitors enhance incretin levels to lower glucose. Biguanides, primarily metformin, reduce hepatic glucose production. Insulins manage blood sugar directly by facilitating glucose uptake. SGLT-2 inhibitors prevent glucose reabsorption in kidneys, promoting excretion. α-Glucosidase inhibitors slow carbohydrate absorption in the intestines. Additionally, the "Other" category encompasses emerging therapies and alternative treatments that address diabetes management and improve patient outcomes, broadening treatment options.</p></p>
