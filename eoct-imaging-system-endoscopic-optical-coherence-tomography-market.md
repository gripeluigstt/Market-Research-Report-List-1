<p><p>Endoscopic Optical Coherence Tomography (EOCT) is an advanced imaging system used in medical diagnostics, particularly for assessing gastrointestinal conditions. The market includes two main types: balloon optical probes and balloon-less optical probes. Balloon optical probes employ a balloon to stabilize the probe during imaging, providing enhanced clarity and depth in tissue visualization. In contrast, balloon-less optical probes allow for more flexible maneuverability within the body, making them suitable for varied anatomical regions without the need for balloon inflation.</p></p>
