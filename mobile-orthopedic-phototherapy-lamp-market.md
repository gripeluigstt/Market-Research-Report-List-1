<p><p>The Mobile Orthopedic Phototherapy Lamp market is categorized into two primary types: Infrared and Visible Light. Infrared lamps use longer wavelengths to penetrate deep into tissues, promoting healing, reducing pain, and improving blood circulation. They are particularly effective for chronic conditions and muscle injuries. In contrast, Visible Light lamps utilize shorter wavelengths to target surface-level skin issues and stimulate cellular activity, aiding in wound healing and tissue repair. Both types serve distinct therapeutic purposes in orthopedic treatments.</p></p>
