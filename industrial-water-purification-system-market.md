<p><strong>The Industrial Water Purification System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ion Exchange</li><li>Filtration</li><li>Distillation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial water purification system market includes three primary types: ion exchange, filtration, and distillation. Ion exchange removes contaminants by replacing them with less harmful ions, making it effective for softening water. Filtration employs physical barriers to separate impurities from water, making it ideal for suspended solids and biological contaminants. Distillation involves boiling water to produce steam, which is then condensed back into liquid, effectively removing dissolved solids and microorganisms. Each method serves specific industrial needs, ensuring clean water supplies.</p></p>
