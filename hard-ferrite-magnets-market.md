<p><ul><li>Sintered Ferrite Magnet</li><li>Bonded Ferrite Magnet</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hard ferrite magnets market is primarily divided into two types: sintered ferrite magnets and bonded ferrite magnets. Sintered ferrite magnets are made through a sintering process that involves high-temperature treatment of powdered materials, resulting in strong and durable magnets, ideal for various industrial applications. Bonded ferrite magnets, on the other hand, are created by mixing ferrite powder with a polymer binder, allowing for flexible shapes and sizes, making them suitable for consumer products and complex geometries.</p></p>
