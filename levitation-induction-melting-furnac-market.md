<p><strong>The Levitation Induction Melting Furnac Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Coreless Induction Furnace</li><li>Cold Crucible Induction Furnace</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Levitation induction melting furnaces utilize electromagnetic forces to melt materials without contact, offering precise control and minimizing contamination. The coreless induction furnace, featuring a coil that directly heats the melt, is ideal for various metals due to its efficiency and simplicity. In contrast, the cold crucible induction furnace uses a conductive crucible at ambient temperature, allowing for the melting of reactive metals while preventing oxidation. Both types are valuable in industries requiring high-purity metal processing and innovation in material science.</p></p>
