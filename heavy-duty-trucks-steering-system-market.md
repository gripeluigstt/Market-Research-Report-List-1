<p><p>The heavy-duty trucks steering system market features two primary types: Electric Power Steering (EPS) and Electro-Hydraulic Power Steering (EHPS). EPS utilizes an electric motor to assist with steering, offering enhanced fuel efficiency and improved responsiveness. In contrast, EHPS combines hydraulic assistance with electric control, allowing for more precise handling and reduced weight, which benefits overall vehicle performance. Both systems aim to enhance driver comfort and safety while optimizing the steering experience in demanding heavy-duty applications.</p></p>
