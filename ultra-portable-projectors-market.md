<p><ul><li>DLP Projectors</li><li>LCOS Projectors</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ultra-portable projectors market can be segmented into three main types: DLP (Digital Light Processing) projectors, LCOS (Liquid Crystal on Silicon) projectors, and others. DLP projectors utilize micro mirrors to produce images, offering vibrant colors and compact designs, making them ideal for portability. LCOS projectors, known for their high resolution and excellent color accuracy, employ liquid crystals, providing superior image quality but often at a higher cost. Other types include LED and laser projectors, catering to various user preferences and needs.</p></p>
